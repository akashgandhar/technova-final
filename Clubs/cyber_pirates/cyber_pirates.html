CyberPirates Club

The CyberPirates club is set with a goal to guide individuals about Information security and cyber awareness which help them gain the necessary knowledge to arm themselves against modern-day computer exploits. Welcome to the CyberPirates Club of Technova - Technical Society, SSET.

Join Club

WHY WORK WITH CYBERPIRATES CLUB?

What We Do?

The club is open to everybody at SET, Sharda University no matter what experience level or major they are. Our aim is to impart skills and knowledge to young minds to reduce the risk of cyber attacks and protection against the unauthorized exploitation of systems, networks and technologies. The club invites enthusiastic students to study about cyber security and achieve success. This club will help start and maintain a culture in the institute which will go a long way in helping students of current and future batches.
