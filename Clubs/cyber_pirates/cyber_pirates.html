WHY WORK WITH CYBERPIRATES CLUB?

What We Do?

We at CyberPirates, a crucial part of Technova, are committed to building a safer society through cybersecurity education. Our objective is to empower individuals by providing them with comprehensive knowledge and skills in cybersecurity, encompassing ethical hacking, penetration testing, and various related domains. We strive to establish a vibrant community that fosters learning, collaboration, and innovation in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity. Through immersive workshops, insightful seminars, and interactive sessions, we aim to equip members with the expertise needed to secure digital assets and networks effectively. Our goal is to cultivate a culture of responsible online conduct and create a robust network of cyber-savvy individuals, contributing to a more secure digital environment for all.

Our Events

Mind Blowing Events!

HACK-A-PHONE

The Hack-a-Phone event was successfully hosted by The Cyberpirates Club. This remarkable gathering occurred on the 12th of June, 2023, and brought together individuals with a keen interest in technology, cybersecurity professionals, and inquisitive minds from diverse backgrounds. The event showcased a captivating speaker, Mr. Pranaav Bhatnagar, who holds the role of Club Co-Lead. Mr. Bhatnagar, with the exceptionally talented Ms. Chetna Talan conducted live demonstrations of phone hacking techniques and provided valuable insights on maintaining personal safety in the digital realm. The Hack-a-Phone event not only imparted knowledge about the vulnerabilities of contemporary smartphones to the participants but also instilled a sense of awareness and responsibility regarding personal cybersecurity. Attendees left the event equipped with a deeper understanding of the potential risks associated with phone hacking and armed with practical solutions to mitigate these threats.
