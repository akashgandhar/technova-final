WHY WORK WITH DATAPOOL CLUB?

What We Do?

To empower students and enthusiasts in the realms of Data Science, AI, ML, and related fields, by curating an environment of learning and exploration. Through organizing workshops, peer-to-peer sessions, and talks by industrial experts, our goal is to bridge the knowledge gap between academia and the industry. By consistently aligning with our vision of being a global beacon of excellence in technical knowledge and our mission of fostering analytical learning, we aim to not only enhance the core competencies of our members but also provide tangible opportunities, like internships, to immerse them in real-world applications. Moreover, by actively promoting interdisciplinary research, innovation, and industry-academia collaborations, we endeavor to infuse a spirit of entrepreneurship, social consciousness, and ethical decision-making, ensuring that our members are well-equipped to address the dynamic challenges of the future.

Our Events

Mind Blowing Events!

Get Your Head in the Cloud & RDS

On August 25th, 2023, "The Cloud and RDS Event" featuring Ms. Monika Rajput, hosted by the Data Pool Club, was a tremendous success. Attendees left both inspired and eager to dive into the ever-changing tech landscape. The event not only provided theoretical knowledge but also practical insights into Cloud technologies, RDS databases, and the real-world applications of innovative tech like AutoML and NLP. Interactive Q&A sessions fostered discussions, building a sense of community and enriching participants' understanding of these technologies' potential.

Kaggle Workshop

The Kaggle Workshop, organized by The Datapool Club, was a resounding success on June 14th, 2023. Led by coordinator Aditi Sachdeva, the event brought together a diverse audience of machine learning enthusiasts, professionals, and curious minds. Renowned speaker Omkar Bhattarai delivered captivating insights into data science, data analysis, visualization, and pre-trained models. Attendees left with valuable skills and a deeper appreciation for Kaggle's potential. The workshop empowered participants to excel in their industries by making data-driven decisions and fostered a strong sense of community through interactive sessions, connecting data enthusiasts and building lasting connections.

Love Babbar Event 2022

Datapool club in collaboration with GitHub club worked together to plan an fabulous event on October 7, 2022 featuring the pious coder, dedicated teacher and passionate vlogger, Love Babbar. The event was a fan favorite among the students of Sharda University and a high engagement was shown from the audience. Interactive Session On Product Management
