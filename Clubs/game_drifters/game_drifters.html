WELCOME TO

Game Drifters Club

Dedicated to cultivating a vibrant gaming community at Sharda University, our objective is to unite passionate gamers, elevate esports excellence, and nurture aspiring game developers. Through thrilling tournaments, immersive gaming experiences, and collaborative game development projects, we aim to foster camaraderie, teamwork, and innovation within the gaming realm, creating an inclusive space where enthusiasts can thrive and push the boundaries of digital entertainment

WHY WORK WITH GAME DRIFTERS CLUB?

What We Do?

Students can learn technical skills of Game Development or get a platform to showcase their gaming talent. Game Drifters Club is a hub for gaming enthusiasts and game developers to learn and share their skills and thoughts. Don't let your gaming hobby stay a hobby, instead make it into your strength, which is possible through this club. Join us to dive deep into the unexplored depths of gaming eutopia. This club is designed to be an inclusive and welcoming community that is open to gamers of all skill levels and backgrounds. Whether you are a casual gamer or a competitive player, you can benefit from the resources and community that Game Drifters Club provides.

Our Events

Mind Blowing Events!

2D&3D Game Development Using Unity

The "2D & 3D Game Development Using Unity" event, which took place on September 15, 2023, was an unquestionable success. Dr. Shiraz Khurana, our esteemed speaker, led participants through the essentials of the Unity game engine and the intricacies of 2D and 3D game development, equipping them with invaluable skills and knowledge. The event's collaborative atmosphere encouraged networking and idea sharing, creating a strong sense of community among game developers. As a result, attendees departed the event with not only enhanced game development skills but also the inspiration to embark on their own creative projects in the world of 2D and 3D game development.

Sharda GDC Valorant Tournament '23

The Valorant Tournament Event is a competitive gaming event that brings together teams of players to compete against each other in the popular online multiplayer game, Valorant. The event is typically hosted either online or in-person and is open to players of all skill levels. During the Valorant Tournament Event, teams will compete in a series of matches, with the winners advancing to the next round until a final champion is crowned. The tournament typically follows a single-elimination format, meaning that teams are eliminated from the tournament once they lose a match.

Webinar of Intro to Game Development

The Introduction to Game Development Event is a workshop that introduces participants to the world of game development. The event was typically aimed at beginners who were interested in learning the basics of game design and programming. During the Introduction to Game Development Event, participants learnt about the different aspects of game development, including game design, programming, art and graphics, and sound design. The event included lectures, interactive sessions, and hands-on exercises to help participants gain practical experience in creating games.

Game Drifters Club
Penta Esports Tournament

The PENTA Esports Event is a professional gaming tournament that showcases some of the best players and teams in the world of competitive gaming. The event is typically held either online or in-person and features a range of different games, including first-person shooters, MOBAs, and battle royales. During the PENTA Esports Event, teams will compete against each other in a series of matches, with the winners advancing to the next round. The tournament typically follows a double-elimination format, meaning that teams are eliminated from the tournament after losing two matches.

Game Drifters Club
AMD BGMI Tournament

Game Drifters Club, in association with AMD, organised an online BGMI Tournament, Week 1 which was sponsored by the latter. BGMI is a free-to-play online battle royale game where several players compete to be the last man standing to win. The event registration started from 23rd March 2022. The match days were divided into two days- Qualifiers and Finals. Over 65 teams, each comprising of 4 players, participated in the event. A prizepool of INR 10,000 was organised for the top 3 teams
