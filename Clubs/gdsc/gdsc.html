Our Events

Mind Blowing Events!

GDSC Kotlin Conf'23

The KotlinConf'23 is an event that looks to invite 4 industry speakers to distribute their knowledge about the Kotlin programming language. This event will introduce the participants to the world of Android development. The participants will get to interact with speakers who have worked on an industry level and gain insight into how real-world projects get developed in companies. Participants will also get to connect with other Kotlin enthusiasts and developers and discover new opportunities. This is the perfect event to network with like-minded individuals and expand your knowledge and skills.

April 20, 2023

Google Developer Student Club
Introductory Session on Women Engineers Program

This session was taken by Kritika Tripathi, a WE scholar. She explained aspiring first year female engineers about the program. She focused on how to apply for the program, eligibility of the program, the various rounds and how to qualify and present yourself in these rounds to go for further selection process. This program is an amazing opportunity for all the young women engineers that will benefit them and help to bridge the gender gap among tech jobs between male and female engineers.

Google CrowdSource 2022

Google Developers Student Club hosted an eye-capturing event on Google CrowdSource for the entire first and second-year students. The event introduced a prolific speaker Nikhil Raichur, the program manager at Google CrowdSource, to give us wonderful insights regarding the CrowdSource program. He explained in an exquisite way that allowed the audience to very much participate in his discussions. He introduced the crowd to the program and talked about the various benefits of taking part in this program. It was an honor to have him and interact with our students.
