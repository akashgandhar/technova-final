WELCOME TO

Pixelence Club

The Pixelence Club is a community of photography/videography enthusiasts who come together to share their passion for photography, videography learn new techniques, and explore new subjects. The club is open to individuals of all skill levels, from beginners to professional photographers/videographers.

WHY WORK WITH PIXELENCE CLUB?

What We Do?

We, at Pixelence Club, aim to impart skills and knowledge to all those who are curious. and have a will to learn. Anybody with the interest in photography, videography or video editing is welcome to join our club. Our club is always present in all the events to make each event a memorable one by taking pictures and videos. We look forward to newcomers and hope to make this club great. We are present at every other event conducted by all the clubs to capture the momements and make it a memorable event.
