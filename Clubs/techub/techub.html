Tech-Hub club aims in providing a community of entrepreneurial driven mindset people & a learning experience to all the members. It trains and promotes individuals who have sparked an interest in entrepreneurship. Welcome to TechHub Club part of Techincal Society - Technova, SSET.

WHY WORK WITH TECH-HUB CLUB?

What We Do?

From hosting startup founders & CEO's to organizing Pitch sessions for Funding, we have come a long way & we look forward to making this community much stronger than before. Tech-Hub brings people from different domains to create a diverse community to help you with Networking. We are available on WhatsApp, Discord, LinkedIn & Instagram to keep our community updated. Some of the key features of this club includes access to mentorship and coaching, educational workshops and seminars, and networking events. Members can also collaborate with other entrepreneurs and receive feedback and support on their business ideas and plans.

Mind Blowing Events!

Career-Debugging

The event featuring social media influencer and senior software engineer at Samsung, Mr. Arsh Goyal, served as an invaluable starting point for individuals beginning their journey in B.Tech and computer science. This engaging talk-show-style event delved into critical topics such as data structures, career planning, job hunting, and profile building. Audience interaction, fueled by the "#AskArshAnything" digital form for questions, added an interactive dimension to the event. Thanks to the collaborative efforts of the team and a highly engaged audience, the event unfolded seamlessly, providing valuable insights into the industry and the nuances of the tech world, leaving a lasting impact on all attendees.
