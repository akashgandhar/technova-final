Welcome To Our Team

The goal of school, guided by the Sharda University vision, is to provide transformational education that produces a skilled workforce of professionals including researchers and innovators of tomorrow with intellectual and technological resources. Our commitment and dedication is to focus on experiential, cooperative and project-based learning that allows our students to continue to adopt, grow and succeed in solving real-world problems. The school provides funding to innovative ideas of the students to develop, products, patents and startups. The school is collaborated with IIA, IEA, Greater Noida Authority and other Industries to ensure benchmarking of programs and activities. The school thrives to establish a partnership with industries, government organizations, & academia and become a collaborative community of faculties, students, staff and alumni to fulfil societal needs with professional ethics. The philosophy of the school is: "By the Students, For the Students, and With the Students"

Prof. (Dr.) Parma Nand

Pro-Vice Chancellor, Sharda University

The School of Engineering & Technology at Sharda University is a dynamic hub offering specialized programs spanning various engineering disciplines, including but not limited to Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Internet of Things, Data Science, and Business Intelligence. In our quest for holistic student development and the promotion of an innovative culture, our Students Activity Clubs, led by student teams and guided by experienced faculty members, transcend program boundaries. These clubs attract students from around the globe, fostering personal growth and excellence across multidisciplinary domains, significantly impacting placement opportunities, startup ventures, national and international competitions, and cutting-edge research outcomes. Over the past three years, these student-led clubs have consistently excelled, leaving a lasting mark on the entire Engineering & Technology landscape.

Prof. (Dr.) Shankar Ramamoorthy

Dean, SSET

Computer Science & Engineering is one of the most vibrant department of Sharda University with varieties of specialized programs in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Internet of Things, Data Science and Business Intelligence. To have holistic development of students of distinct programs of computer science and to grow the innovative culture among the students, Students Activity Clubs are functional. These clubs are headed by the team of students and they are performing in different dimensions of technology under the guidance of specialized faculty members. Several national and international students are contributing to develop themselves and other peers to excel among the multidisciplinary aspects. The club activities strengthens placements, startups, national/international competitions and research outcomes. We are proud to have high aimed and energetic students club performing exceptionally well since last three years. "Best Wishes To All My Dear Students!"

Dr. Sanjeev Kumar Pippal

Professor & HoD, Dept. Of CSE, SSET

The philosophy of the Technical Society has always been to encourage overall development of students. Our aim is to create an environment of collaborative growth, which as a result will lead to mutually uplifting of the community. Each vertical of club under the Technical society(Technova) organizes various events and extra-curricular activities throughout the year, through these Technical Club's events we strive to bring forth the latent potential and abilities of all of our students. The objective of the students club is to sensitize the technological updating, by enabling the students to participate in various activities like, codathon, hackathons, peer-to-peer, entrepreneurial activities, programming challenges, app development, certifications, industrial challenges and many other activities. The student's club strengthens the presentation, verbal and communication skills, leadership qualities and confidence. We believe in: "Nurturing Technical & Interpersonal Skills"

Dr. Rani Astya

Asst. Professor, Dept. of CSE, SSET
